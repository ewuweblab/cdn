Quick Tips:
There is no fold. Make the vertical canvas as tall as possible. Then design. Don't squeeze your design into random heights.
It's not page layout. Don't place elements like a poster.
Use. A. Grid. Set a grid, then use it. It's a pixel based medium and grids are good.
